The Best Kdramas According to Justine

My top 3 kdramas of all-time

3. Missing You

This drama is one of the very first melodrama- genre series that I watched. It was starring by Yoo-chun parK, Yu-Sung ho, Kim-so hyun, Yoo-Eun hye. The drama is very sad. It is about the that has been replaced by revenge because of the incident happened when they were young. I actually watched the first four episodes for so many times. This drama is perfect to watch during rainy season.

2. Cheese in the trap

I watched a lot of kdramas that I forget most of them but this one is one of the kdramas I watched when I was a teenager. I seldom go outside to play with my friends because I rather choose to stay at home and binged-watch my favorite series. It is about a college student who has to juggle between earning a living and pursuing studies.

1. I hear your voice

This is the first Kdrama that I watched. It is starring by lee jong suk, lee bo young and lee da hee. Kim so hyun is also a cast of this show and played the young lee bo young. It is a drama about justice and love.
